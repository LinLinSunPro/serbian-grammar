# Vocabulary: Family (Moja Porodica)

## Family Members (Članovi Porodice)

(Source: Image 2, Diagram)

* **Ja**: Me
* **Mama**: Mom
* **Tata**: Dad
* **Roditelji**: Parents
* **Brat**: Brother
* **Sestra**: Sister
* **Deda**: Grandfather
* **Baba**: Grandmother
* **Muž**: Husband
* **Žena**: Wife
* **Sin**: Son
* **Ćerka / Kći**: Daughter
* **Unuk**: Grandson
* **Unuka**: Granddaughter
* **Snaja**: Daughter-in-law / Sister-in-law (Brother's wife)
* **Zet**: Son-in-law / Brother-in-law (Sister's husband) - *Implied context often pairs with Snaja*

## Relations (Odnosi)

* **Ana i Petar**: deca (children) -> **Jana i Andrej**
* **Snaja**: Sister-in-law (brother's wife)
* **Brat**: Brother
