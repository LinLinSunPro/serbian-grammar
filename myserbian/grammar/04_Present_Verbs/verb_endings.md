# Prezent Glagola (-I / -E)

Present Tense of verbs ending in -I and -E.

| Person | Raditi (to work) | Učiti (to study) | Govoriti (to speak) | Živeti (to live) | Voleti (to love) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **JA** | radi-m | uči-m | govori-m | živi-m | voli-m |
| **TI** | radi-š | uči-š | govori-š | živi-š | voli-š |
| **ON/ONA/ONO** | radi | uči | govori | živi | voli |
| **MI** | radi-mo | uči-mo | govori-mo | živi-mo | voli-mo |
| **VI** | radi-te | uči-te | govori-te | živi-te | voli-te |
| **ONI/ONE/ONA** | rad-e | uč-e | govor-e | živ-e | vol-e |

*Note: The 3rd person plural for these verbs ends in **-e**.*
