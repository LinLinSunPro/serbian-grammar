# Question Formation & Question Words

(Extracted from Sentence Structure Ex 18 Qu 4, 6, 8)

* **Kako** se zovete? (How)
* **Da li** ste vi Kinez? (Are you / Yes-No Question Particle)

## Common Question Structures

* **Da li...** + Verb? (Yes/No Question)
  * *Da li si ti student?*
* **Ko...**? (Who)
  * *Ko je to?*
* **Šta...**? (What)
  * *Šta je to?*
* **Gde...**? (Where)
  * *Gde živiš?*
* **Odakle...**? (From where)
  * *Odakle si?*
* **Kako...**? (How)
  * *Kako si?*
