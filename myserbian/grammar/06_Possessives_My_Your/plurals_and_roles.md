# Grammar: Plurals of Nouns (Množina Imenica)

## Rule: Professions (Zanimanja)

(Source: Image 5)

* **Masculine (Muški rod):** ON (Novinar) -> ONI (Novinar**i**)
  * Ends in consonant -> Add **-i**
* **Feminine (Ženski rod):** ONA (Novinarka) -> ONE (Novinark**e**)
  * Ends in -a -> Change to **-e**
